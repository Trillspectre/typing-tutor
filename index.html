How to Take the Typing Test
  1. Select a Difficulty: Choose Easy, Medium, or Hard from the dropdown menu.
  2. Read the Sample Text: A random text will appear for you to type. The words will be highlighted as you type: blue for correct, red for incorrect.
  3. Start the Test: Click the Start button, or press Space or Tab (unless your cursor is in the typing box).
  4. Type the Text: Enter the sample text in the input area. Only type while the test is running.
  5. Stop the Test: Click the Stop button, or press Enter while in the typing box, when you finish typing.
  6. View Results: Your time, WPM (words per minute), and level will be shown in the results area.
  7. Retry: Click Retry to try again with a new random text.
  • Only words that exactly match the sample text are counted as correct.
  • WPM is calculated based on the number of correct words and your time.
  • The test area and results are fully responsive for all devices.
